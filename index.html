Data Science in Educational Research

For the past year, I have been a part of the Computational Creativity research group at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln under the guidance of Dr. Soh Leen-Kiat. As a group, we take special interest in student behavior and its implications on learning motivation and ability. Our ultimate goal is to be able to provide targeted intervention for students in need based on the textual data we collect from their responses to Creative Thinking exercises.
